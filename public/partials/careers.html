Chemist Position

American Boronite Corporation (“Boronite”) is an advanced materials company manufacturing Boron Nitride Nanotube continuous yarns and tapes. These materials have the potential to have significant impact on a wide range of industries from energy to advanced structures.

We are seeking a hands-on, motivated, talented, and ambitious multidisciplinary chemist to help in the detailed design, optimization, and operation of our state-of-the-art nanotube production processes. In this position you will:

  • Help optimize the chemistry for the synthesis of boron nitride.
  • Help optimize and design the synthesis processes specifically for Boronite’s proprietary fuels.
  • Assist in the overall design and operation of tape and yarn production systems.
  • Assist in the development of a process to clean boron nitride nanotube yarns in real time.
  • Work with Boronite’s other engineers and materials scientists to develop and characterize properties.
  • Articulate your designs and present results to the board of technical advisors and to the research group and potentially at public meetings.
  • Contribute to proposals.
  • Potentially provide supervision to one or more junior engineers or technicians.

Qualified candidates have a a PhD in chemistry, organic chemistry, chemical engieering or a related discipline, work well with others, and have close familiarity with chemical synthesis processes. Experience with nanomaterials is a plus.

Boronite offers a competitive salary and generous equity incentives for its employees.

Interested candidates should submit a cover letter, a CV, and names and contact information for three references to info@boronite.com. US Citizenship is required.
