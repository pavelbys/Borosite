An almost endless list of potential applications exist for BNNTs based on their unique mechanical, electro-mechanical, thermal, radiation-shielding, and high fracture energy properties. They are ideally suited towards applications in extreme environments such as space, but are also ideal replacements for materials used in many terrestrial technologies. Potential applications include: structural health monitoring, structural reinforcement, structural composites, ballistic protection, thermal management, radiation protection, actuation, and multifunctional combinations of the above.  Furthermore, if non-toxicity is confirmed in-vivo, this would lead to a host of biomedical and environmental applications as well.
